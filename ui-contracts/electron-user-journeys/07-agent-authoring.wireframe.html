Review notes

Benefits, tradeoffs, open questions
Benefits

Authors understand what they are changing. Publish readiness becomes a product state, not a hidden validation result.

Tradeoffs

More validation copy can make authoring feel strict. Expressive labels may require onboarding.

Open questions

Should heartbeat behavior be hidden from first-time authors until advanced mode?
